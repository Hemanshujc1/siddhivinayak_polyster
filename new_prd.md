This is a very good direction, but I would make one major change.

Your current SRS is trying to be **a company website**.

I think it should become **the definitive Polyester Yarn Knowledge Platform with your company built into it.**

That is what will generate leads.

A buyer searching

- "75D 36F vs 75D 72F"
- "What is NIM yarn?"
- "DTY vs FDY"
- "Best yarn for socks"
- "150D/48F means"

should land on **your website**, learn everything, and then naturally contact you.

For more than 15 years, Siddhi Vinayak Polyester has been supplying premium polyester yarn solutions to textile manufacturers across India.

- Product Finder
- Compare Products
- Request Quote

- Yarn Basics
- Technical Guides

---

I also recommend updating the SRS to remove products you don't actually manufacture (such as ATY, spun yarns, flame-retardant yarns, etc.) unless you genuinely sell them. Focus the site on your real business—**manufacturing DTY (PTY) and trading POY and FDY from leading mills**—while making it the most comprehensive and user-friendly polyester yarn knowledge resource online. That combination will build credibility and make the website a much stronger lead-generation tool.
